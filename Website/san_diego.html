San Diego

Anybody I know who's from California has said that San Diego is their most favorite place in the state. With mostly the same weather of Los Angeles, it's one of the most gorgeous places in the United States.

Pros

  • Tons of stuff to do
  • Excellent weather
  • Beaches are some of the best
  • Less people and traffic than LA
  • Great Mexican food
  • The Zoo!!!

Cons

  • Not too sure about the job market down there
  • It's a little drier down there, which could be a good or a bad thing
  • A little pricier than LA depending on where you live.
