San Jose

San Jose is considered the capital of Silicon Valley. This one is in a different location than the other two, being that it's in northern California rather than the south. It's lesser know than it's neighbor San Fransisco, but the city still has a lot to offer.

Pros

  • The tech job market is fabulous
  • A huge amount of stuff in the city is really high tech
  • The weather is milder than Souther Cali, but still has more sun than SF

Cons

  • EXPENSIVE!!!!
  • I heard there are some pretty bad neighborhoods in some parts of the city
  • No beaches :( (except for the bay)
