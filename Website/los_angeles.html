Los Angeles

The City of Angles probably has the best weather year round out of all of the places on this list (or so I've heard). It's probably most famous for the Hollywood movie industry, but I've heard that there is a lot more than meets the eye to this city.

Pros

  • Year round sunny weather
  • Gorgeous beaches and mountains
  • Plenty of stuff to do
  • Disneyland!!
  • Maybe see a celebrity or two?

Cons

  • Smog
  • Traffic
  • Overpopulation
  • Dangerous areas
